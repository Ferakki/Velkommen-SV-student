Food and drinks

On campus, there are multitude of cafes run by the student community Sammen. The following is a collection of some social science cafes the students at the faculty can use. Her er et par stykker vi SV-studenter kan benytte oss av. Student discounts is applied at the desk.

SV Kaffebar

SV Kaffebar

Our very own cafe is located on the social science faculty (Lauritz Meltzers hus) and is a place for hungry social scientists. The cafe was refurbished in 2020, and is now one of the finer cafes compared to the other cafes at other faculties. With fine places and a great selection of food, in addition to excellent service from the employees, you are most definetly getting a good experience. PS: Ask if you rather want brew coffe if you are not facinated by what comes out of the coffemachine.

Address:

Fosswinckels gate 6

Opening hours:

mon-thur(fri): 08:00-16:00 (15:00)

Studentsenteret

Studentsenteret

Studensenteret is found at the top of the hill on Nygårdshøyden. This is a commonplace for all students at the university, but is very popular for social science students because of the close proximity to the faculty. Studentsenteret's cafe is what tops everything by its comprehensive offer from a simple breakfast to varm dinners, and a nice dessert on top of that. Stay updated by checking out menu of the week whenever you get cravings for some good food after a long day with school instead of making something yourself. Recommended!

address:

Parkveien 1

Openings hours:

mon-fri: 08:30-17:00
