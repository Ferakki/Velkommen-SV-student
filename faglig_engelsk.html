The study aspects of the social sience faculty

Do you need a place to focus without the disturbance and distractions at home? Do you need a place where everyone is studying? No need to worry, the social science faculty offers studyhalls and a library where you can focus on exactly what you need. These halls can be used whenever you need to fully focus.

Studyhalls: A place for concentration and focus

Studyhalls is a place where students can gather and sit in a calm environment and study. Every students are able to book big rooms or smaller rooms with few available seats. The studyhalls are located around the social science faculty thats only walking distance away.

The social science library

Down the street from the studentcenter, right next to the social science faculty, you can find the social science library. The students have access to litterature and resources for every subject. In addition to that, there is even places to sitt and study if you want some peace and quite with others. The library is a common library for three faculties; The social science, the psycological faculty, and the faculty for art, music and design.
