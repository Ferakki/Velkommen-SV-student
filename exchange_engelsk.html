Welcome, exchange student!

You must be new here! This page will give you everything you need to get familiar with the exhange student program. As a new student, there will be arrangements and group activities hosted by the faculty or leading studentgroups who will help you. This page will cover some of the things you can look out for.

What the social science faculty offers

The faculty offers a selection of arrangements, studyhalls and cafees hosted by other students and administration on the faculty, which you as a exchange students can join in on. We know that information about these things can be overwhelming, so we made this website that gives you all the essential information you need to get familiar with the faculty. The following are some arrangements you as a exchange student can participate in before semesterstart.

Norwegian courses

The university offers norwegian courses for exchange students usually before the start of a semester. The course can be taken in July before autumn, and in December before spring semester. The courses offered have different degrees of difficulty, and one has to pass the previous before moving on to next. Read more about the different courses.

Mentorweek

Also called sponsor week, is a full week dedicated for students to get to know each other. Usually during the first week of the semester, the week consists of arrangements, parties and activities ranging from bar to bar, quizzes, physical activities, and clubs. Bringing alcohol is of course optional and is not a necessity to get to know each other. Getting to know each other during this week is crucial that you can support each other during the semester.
