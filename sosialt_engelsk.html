Social - Commonareas for the faculty

General information

On the Social Science faculty (SV), there is a lot to partake in. Everyone on the faculty is invited and is able to take apart in the activites. The arrangements is hosted by individuall organizational and activities for each institution and major.

The student committee

A selection that effects everyone at the faculty is the student committee (SU) which is the political student organization on the social science faculty. Their task is to improve the daily life of a student at the faculty. They deal with political subjects and welfare policy at the faculty. The committee works as a intermediary between the 11 subjectcommittee's and other student organization on the faculty. If you want to learn more about the student committee, check out their Facebook.

The subject committee

As mentioned above, there are a total of 7 subject committee's at the social science faculty. They work together to create a comfortable and fun environment on the institutions. The subject committee hosts arrangements both internally and for students on the institution. Visit the respected subject committee's facebook by cliking one of the links below.

The 7 subject committee's:

  • Administration and organizational science
  • Geography
  • Information and media science
  • Comparative policy
  • Social Anthropology
  • Sociology
  • Social economics

Diskuterbar

Our own studentbar!

In 2019, the faculty got its own studentbar, and the name was Diskuterbar. The bar is originally located in Ulrikkes Phils hus, but is moved (only a few meters) to 4. floor on Christies gate 18 Because of the original place is under renovation. This is the popular place to gather after a long day at school. Diskuterbar hosts quizzes each week, in addition to other arrangements. More information can be found on their Facebook page.
